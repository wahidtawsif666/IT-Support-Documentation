# Business Documentation

## Overview
Business processes, workflows, and operational procedures documentation.

## Documentation Types

### Process Documentation
- Sales Process
- Procurement Process
- Inventory Management
- Customer Service
- Financial Management
- HR Procedures

### Standard Operating Procedures (SOPs)
- Daily operations
- Month-end closing
- Quarter planning
- Annual reviews

### Policies & Standards
- Company policies
- Quality standards
- Compliance requirements
- Risk management

### Forms & Templates
- Purchase orders
- Invoices
- Contracts
- Reports

## Process Flow Format
```
1. Process Start
2. Input Requirements
3. Process Steps
4. Decision Points
5. Output/Result
6. Process End
```

## Documentation Schedule
- Reviews: Quarterly
- Updates: As needed
- Approvals: Department heads
